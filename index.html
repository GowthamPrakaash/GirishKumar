Products

Payment Gateway

Our Payment Gateway offers seamless and secure transactions for businesses of all sizes. It supports multiple payment methods and ensures a smooth checkout experience for your customers.

Payment Link

With Payment Link, you can easily send payment requests to your customers via email, SMS, or social media. It is a simple and efficient way to collect payments without needing a website or app.

POS Machine

Our POS Machine integrates seamlessly with your business operations, providing a reliable solution for in-store payments. It supports various payment methods, including contactless and mobile payments.

Soft POS

Soft POS transforms your smartphone or tablet into a powerful payment terminal, allowing you to accept payments anywhere, anytime. This solution is perfect for businesses on the move, offering flexibility and convenience without the need for additional hardware.

Tabby

Tabby is our innovative payment solution designed to enhance the shopping experience. With Tabby, customers can split their purchases into manageable payments, making it easier for them to buy what they need while managing their budget effectively.

Tamara

Tamara is a cutting-edge payment service that provides flexible payment options for customers. With Tamara, businesses can offer their customers the ability to pay later or split payments, enhancing customer satisfaction and boosting sales.
